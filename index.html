About Me

I have 15 years of experience as a graphic designer in a corporate environment. This includes professional digital and traditional illustration skills and proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite. More recently, I've been developing web applications using traditional front-end languages (html, css, javascript) and also with javascript frameworks like React. I've also built back-end applications using Postgres and Sequelize.

A native Texan, I now call Smyrna, GA home with my lovely wife and two children.
